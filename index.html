Our Story

Coffee is a craft, not a commodity.

We opened Brewed in 2018 with one idea: that a cup of coffee should taste like where it came from. Every bean we use is traceable to a specific farm, washed or natural processed, and roasted in small batches each week.

Our baristas train for 6 months before they touch a portafilter. That's not extra — it's the minimum to do this right.

🫘

Direct Trade

We pay above fair-trade rates directly to farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Coorg.

🔥

Weekly Roast

Beans roasted every Monday in-house. What you drink was roasted days ago, not months.

📖

Work-friendly

Fast Wi-Fi, power at every seat, no time limit. Stay as long as your work needs.

🌱

Zero Waste

Compostable cups, spent grounds go to local gardens. We track what we throw away.
